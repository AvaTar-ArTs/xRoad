   - Update pricing if needed
   - Refine targeting/category

**Friday**:
1. Deploy updated version
   - Update Codester product page

### What Gets Learned & Applied

**Pattern 1: Title Keywords Drive CTR**
```
Finding: Products with "API" in title: 3.2% CTR
Finding: Products without "API": 1.8% CTR
Implication: Buyers search for "API"
Action: Update all product titles to lead with API/integration keywords
Impact: Apply to all 72+ products, expected +1% CTR overall
```

**Pattern 2: Social Proof Improves Conversions**
```
Finding: Products with 3+ testimonials: 3.5% conversion
Finding: Products with 0 testimonials: 1.8% conversion
Implication: Reviews matter for conversion
Action: Prioritize collecting testimonials, highlight in descriptions
Impact: Could improve overall conversion 50%+
```

**Pattern 3: Email Frequency vs Unsubscribe Rate**
```
Finding: 2x weekly emails: 28% unsubscribe/week
Finding: 1x weekly emails: 8% unsubscribe/week
Finding: 2x monthly emails: 3% unsubscribe/week
Implication: Less frequent is better for retention
Action: Move to bi-weekly email schedule
Impact: Keep more subscribers, better LTV
```
