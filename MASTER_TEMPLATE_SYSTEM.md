## üí° Optimization Tips

### For Codester (Organic Discovery)
1. **SEO Titles**: Lead with keywords (e.g., "Python Claude AI Integration" not "My New Tool")
2. **Descriptions**: First 100 characters MUST hook (problem statement)
3. **Screenshots**: Show before/after results
4. **Reviews**: Request from first 5 buyers immediately
5. **Pricing**: A/B test within $35-65 range, adjust based on views

### For Gumroad (Email-Driven)
1. **Free Tier**: Always enable (email capture)
2. **Sequences**: Send within 24 hours of purchase
3. **Video**: Include 5-minute setup video link
4. **Social Proof**: Use customer quotes in description
5. **Updates**: Post monthly updates (shows active maintenance)

### For Lemon Squeezy (Premium)
1. **Positioning**: Enterprise/professional messaging
2. **Support**: Emphasize included support tier
3. **License Keys**: Highlight team/business use
4. **Testimonials**: Use highest-value customers
5. **Pricing Tiers**: Consider offering starter/pro/enterprise levels
