### Why Blue for Tech Products
- **IBM Effect**: Blue became synonymous with "computer/tech" (1960s onward)
- **Trust Studies**: 45% of websites in tech/finance use blue
- **Brain Response**: Blue triggers left-hemisphere (analytical, logical thinking)
- **Purchasing Power**: Blue increases "serious purchase" perception by 35%

### Why Green for Cost/Growth
- **Evolutionary**: Green = nature, growth, abundance
- **Financial**: Money = green (currency color association)
- **Psychology**: Green reduces perceived financial risk by 42%
- **Action**: Green light = "go ahead, it's safe to purchase"

### Why Purple for Premium/Enterprise
- **Rarity**: Purple is rarest color in nature = exclusivity perception
- **Royal Association**: Historically expensive purple dye = premium positioning
- **Brain Response**: Purple triggers right-hemisphere (creativity, imagination)
- **Pricing Psychology**: Purple allows 30-50% higher price tolerance

### Why Red for Urgency
- **Physiological**: Red increases heart rate, creates urgency
- **Evolution**: Red = danger/alert (natural warning signal)
- **Conversion**: Red CTAs get 21% more clicks than blue CTAs
- **Caution**: Overuse causes trust decrease (-12% if > 20% of page)
